Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my college attendance percentage?

Divide the number of classes you attended by the total classes held, then multiply by 100. For example, if 50 classes were held and you attended 40, your attendance is (40/50) × 100 = 80%. Our free calculator does this instantly.

How many classes can I bunk and still maintain 75% attendance?

Use our bunk calculator: enter your total and attended classes, set your target to 75%, and it will show how many classes you can safely skip while maintaining the required attendance.

How to calculate CGPA from SGPA?

CGPA is the simple average of all your semester SGPAs. Enter each semester's SGPA in our calculator, and it computes your cumulative CGPA along with the equivalent percentage (CGPA × 10).

Does this tool work offline?

Yes! College Toolkit is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Once loaded, it works fully offline. You can also install it on your phone's home screen for quick access.
