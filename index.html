Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my college attendance percentage?

Attendance % = (Classes Attended ÷ Total Classes) × 100. For example, if 50 classes were held and you attended 40, your attendance is 80%. Use bunkcalculator.online to calculate instantly.

How many classes can I bunk and still maintain 75% attendance?

Enter your total and attended classes, set target to 75%, and our bunk calculator shows exactly how many classes you can safely skip while maintaining the required attendance percentage.

How to calculate CGPA from SGPA?

CGPA = Sum of all SGPAs ÷ Number of semesters. For example, SGPAs of 8.5, 9.0, 8.0 give CGPA = 8.5. Percentage = CGPA × 10 = 85%.

What is the minimum attendance required in Indian colleges?

Most Indian colleges require 75% minimum attendance to sit for exams. Some may require 65% or 80%. Use our calculator to check if you meet the requirement.

How to convert CGPA to percentage?

Multiply CGPA by 10. A CGPA of 8.5 = 85%. Some universities use different conversion factors — check your institution's rules.
